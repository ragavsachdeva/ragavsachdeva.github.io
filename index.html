Ragav Sachdeva

Hi! I am a fourth year PhD student in the Visual Geometry Group at the University of Oxford, supervised by Prof. Andrew Zisserman. I did my undergraduate degree in computer science at the University of Adelaide, where I did my honours thesis with Prof. Gustavo Carneiro, and worked at Google and Microsoft as a SWE intern.
